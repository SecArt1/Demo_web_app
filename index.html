Who We Are

Whole Cyber Human Initiative is a Service-Disabled Veteran Owned Non-profit built on a community foundation to provide, promote, and evaluate free to inexpensive Cybersecurity Education built on the National Institute Cybersecurity Education (NICE) practical skilling.

Founded by Retired Navy Veteran Paul Cummings, along with Leah McLean, Chris Foulon, Alex Rhodes, and our founding Board of Advisors, we are a workforce development research organization with nearly 300 years combined Industry Experience.

Our Vision

Establishment of a true Trade School to unify Department of Education and Labor to build pathways to secure our next 20 years and beyond.

Our Mission

Provide a free tier or paid cohort tier immersive, self-paced curriculum to transitioning service members, first responders, and anyone around the globe.

Our Values

We believe that in cybersecurity nobody should be left behind. We foster an all-inclusiveness and treat everyone as family regardless of race, gender identity, sexual preference, religion, or creed.

Our Core Curriculum

A comprehensive 380-hour program broken into two strategic parts, designed for both technical and non-technical transitions

1

Non-Technical Transition

4-5 Days Estimated

Candidates research and digest the 52 roles presented by the National Initiative for Cybersecurity Careers and Study (NICE). At completion, identify 3-5 roles that closely align with your interests.

  • 52 NICE Cybersecurity Role Analysis
  • Myers-Briggs Personality Assessment
  • Cyber Aptitude Assessment by US CyberKnights
  • Career Path Identification
  • Role Alignment Evaluation
2

Technical & Non-Technical

380 Hours - 12 Weeks Estimated

Begin curated training with education content from industry-leading partners including CISCO, Microsoft, AWS, RangeForce, EvolveSecurity, AttackIQ, and more.

  • Evolve Academy - Intro to Cyber, Linux, Networking
  • CISCO - Network Security & Configuration
  • FortiNet - Certified Fundamentals & Associate
  • Qualys - Vulnerability Management & EDR
  • AttackIQ - MITRE Learning & Purple Team
  • RangeForce - SIEM, Threat Hunting, Home Labs
  • Microsoft - AZ-900 & AZ-104 Training
  • AWS - Cloud Practitioner Certification
  • CISA/DHS - Industrial Control Systems (ICS/OT)
  • Two Gamified CTF Experiences
