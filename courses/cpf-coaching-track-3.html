About This Elite Conference Track

This is the elite Track 3 of the CPF Coaching virtual career conference series, specifically designed for senior cybersecurity and technology professionals who are ready to advance to executive and C-suite positions.

Building on the foundation and intermediate tracks, Track 3 focuses on executive leadership, strategic thinking, and the advanced career development strategies needed to excel at the highest levels of technology and cybersecurity leadership.

In support of Boots to Books and Whole Cyber Human Initiative Non-profits, this conference provides the strategic insights and executive coaching needed for senior professionals to make the leap to executive leadership roles.
