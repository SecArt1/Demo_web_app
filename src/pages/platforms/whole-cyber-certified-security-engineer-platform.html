Certification Levels

Our comprehensive certification program offers multiple levels of expertise validation, from foundational knowledge to advanced specializations. Choose the path that aligns with your career goals and demonstrate your cybersecurity expertise to employers worldwide.

Associate Level
WCSE-A
Entry-level certification for cybersecurity professionals with foundational knowledge and skills in security principles, risk management, and basic technical implementations.
Requirements:
  • Complete Foundation Track (60 hours)
  • Pass Associate Assessment (70% minimum)
  • Complete 2 practical projects
  • Professional portfolio submission
Professional Level
WCSE-P
Advanced certification for experienced professionals with deep technical expertise and leadership capabilities in cybersecurity operations and strategy.
Requirements:
  • Hold WCSE-A certification
  • Complete Advanced Track (80 hours)
  • Pass Professional Assessment (80% minimum)
  • Complete capstone project
  • Peer review participation
Expert Level
WCSE-E
Master-level certification for cybersecurity leaders and subject matter experts with specialized knowledge and proven ability to design and implement enterprise security.
Requirements:
  • Hold WCSE-P certification
  • Complete Expert Track (100 hours)
  • Pass Expert Assessment (85% minimum)
  • Lead capstone project
  • Industry mentorship role
  • Continuing education commitment

Assessment Components

  • Technical Knowledge Exam: Comprehensive assessment covering all certification domains
  • Practical Skills Demonstration: Hands-on lab exercises and problem-solving scenarios
  • Capstone Project: Independent project demonstrating applied knowledge and skills
  • Professional Portfolio: Collection of work samples and professional accomplishments
  • Peer Review: Evaluation and feedback from industry professionals
  • Continuing Education Plan: Commitment to ongoing professional development
