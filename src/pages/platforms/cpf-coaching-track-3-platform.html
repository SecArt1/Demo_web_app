About This Executive Track

Welcome to Track 3 of the CPF Coaching program - the Executive Leadership Track. This premier track is designed for current and aspiring C-level cybersecurity executives who want to master the art of strategic leadership in cybersecurity.

What You'll Master:

  • C-Suite cybersecurity leadership and board-level communication
  • Strategic risk management and digital transformation leadership
  • Organizational culture development and change management
  • Strategic partnerships and vendor relationship management
  • Global cybersecurity operations and international compliance
  • Thought leadership development and industry influence

Prerequisites:

  • Completion of CPF Coaching Tracks 1 & 2
  • 5+ years of cybersecurity leadership experience
  • Current senior management or executive role
  • Experience with strategic planning and budgeting

Executive Benefits:

  • 1:1 executive coaching sessions
  • Peer networking with other C-level executives
  • Board presentation practice and feedback
  • Strategic planning workshop participation
  • Industry thought leadership opportunities
