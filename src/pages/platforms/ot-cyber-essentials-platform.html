What is OT Cybersecurity?

15 minutes 0 views June 24, 2025

Lesson Overview

This lesson introduces the fundamentals of Operational Technology (OT) cybersecurity, specifically tailored for non-electrical engineers. You'll learn the key differences between OT and IT security, understand common industrial control systems, and discover why OT security requires specialized knowledge and approaches.

Learning Objectives

  • Understand the fundamental differences between OT and IT cybersecurity
  • Identify common types of industrial control systems
  • Recognize unique security challenges in OT environments
  • Learn basic OT security terminology and concepts
  • Understand the business impact of OT security incidents
