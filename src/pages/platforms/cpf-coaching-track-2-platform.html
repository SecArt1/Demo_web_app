About This Lesson

Welcome to Track 2 of the CPF Coaching program. This advanced track focuses on leadership development, executive-level career strategies, and team management skills essential for senior cybersecurity professionals.

  • Leadership skills development and team management
  • Professional networking strategies for senior roles
  • Executive search and salary negotiation techniques
  • Personal branding for cybersecurity leaders

Prerequisites:
