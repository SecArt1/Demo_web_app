About This Course

This foundational course is designed to help you move beyond generic career aspirations to discovering specific IT and cybersecurity roles that align with your interests, skills, and career goals.

The Problem We're Solving

Too often in this sector, we as leaders and mentors hear the generic statement "I want to do cybersecurity." The problem is that many people only know the industry name because they never had someone sit down with them to research the actual roles available.

The "I Want to Do Cybersecurity" Problem

Many people only know the industry name because they never had someone sit down with them to research the actual roles available.

Lack of Proper Guidance

Many mentors and educators sadly push certification bootcamps or generic college programs because they don't know how to help people discover specific career paths.

Our Human-Centered Solution

This course is designed to combat that problem, with you as the candidate in mind. We are focused on bringing the human back into the equation.

You Are More Than Statistics

We believe every person has unique strengths, interests, and circumstances that should guide their career path.
