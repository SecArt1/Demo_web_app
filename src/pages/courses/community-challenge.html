About This Course

This donation-based course is designed to get you deep in the trenches as an IT/Cybersecurity practitioner. It encompasses a mixture of step-by-step instruction for installing and configuring essential cybersecurity tools while challenging you to use the tools to accomplish five comprehensive tasks.

Tools You'll Set Up

VirtualBox
Wireshark
Nessus Free Edition
Windows Dev VM
FTK Imager
Autopsy

Reports You'll Create

Vulnerability Assessment/Blue Team Assessment

Comprehensive security evaluation and defensive analysis

Red Team Assessment

Offensive security testing and penetration analysis

Incident Response with Digital Forensics

Including Chain of Custody Report and forensic analysis

Post Red Team Clean-up

System restoration and security hardening procedures

GRC Business Impact Report

With PO&M and 3 Courses of Actions for business continuity

Course Approach

This course is designed for any level and to challenge you to solicit help from the community for the adjacent reporting styles. You will be building each report leading up to a Combo Report, all of which you can showcase on your LinkedIn and highlight your knowledge, skills, and capabilities.
