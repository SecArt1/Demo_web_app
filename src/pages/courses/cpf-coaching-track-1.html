About This Conference Track

The year is 2023 and CPF Coaching is hosting a comprehensive technical career virtual conference focused on helping professionals flourish in their tech and cybersecurity careers throughout 2023.

This is Track 1 of the conference series, designed to provide foundational career development strategies and practical guidance for career seekers, career growth seekers, tech enthusiasts, and curious individuals.

In support of Boots to Books and Whole Cyber Human Initiative Non-profits, this virtual conference brings together expert insights and actionable advice for advancing your career in the technology sector.
