About This Course

In this course, Ken Underhill breaks down the mystery we know as "Job Interview". Through comprehensive modules, you'll gain the knowledge and confidence needed to excel in cybersecurity job interviews.

What You'll Learn

Through the duration of the course, Ken will touch on:

The Famous Question

Learn how to handle the most common and challenging interview questions with confidence and authenticity.

Mental Game

Master the psychological aspects of interviewing, including confidence building and anxiety management.

Interviewer Mind

Understand what interviewers are really looking for and how to position yourself as the ideal candidate.

Interview Bias

Recognize and navigate unconscious biases that may affect the interview process.

Behavioral Based Interview Questions

Master the STAR method and excel at behavioral interviews with compelling storytelling.

Self Sabotage

Identify and overcome common self-defeating behaviors that can derail your interview success.

About Your Instructor

Ken Underhill

Cybersecurity Interview Expert

Ken Underhill is a recognized expert in cybersecurity career development and interview preparation. With years of experience in both interviewing candidates and coaching professionals, Ken provides invaluable insights into the interview process.
