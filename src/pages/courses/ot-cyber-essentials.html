About This Course

This course provides a comprehensive introduction to the critical field of Operational Technology (OT) cybersecurity, tailored specifically for non-electrical engineers.

Course Focus

Participants will gain foundational knowledge of OT systems such as Industrial Control Systems (ICS), including their components, operational principles, and the unique cybersecurity challenges they present.

OT Security Fundamentals

Learn the basics of securing operational technology systems and industrial control systems.

Threat Detection

Understand how to identify and respond to cyber threats in OT environments.

Network Architecture

Master network segmentation strategies and secure OT network design principles.

Legacy System Vulnerabilities

Identify and mitigate security risks in older industrial systems and equipment.
