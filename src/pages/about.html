About Whole Cyber Human Initiative

Empowering veterans, military families, and first responders with comprehensive cybersecurity education through innovative, accessible, and industry-vetted training programs.

Our Mission & Vision

Building a stronger cybersecurity workforce through accessible education and comprehensive training programs.

Our Mission

To provide complimentary, immersive, self-paced cybersecurity programs in support of transitioning service members, veterans, military families, and first responders while offering tier-based cohorts globally.

Our Vision

A world where veterans, military dependents, and first responders can access free or inexpensive upskilling before investing in accelerated bootcamps, creating better pathways to cybersecurity careers.

Our Values

Accessibility, excellence, community support, and industry relevance. We believe in empowering individuals through education and creating force multipliers in cybersecurity.

Our Story

Whole Cyber Human Initiative was founded by retired Navy veteran Paul Cummings, Leah McLean, and a distinguished founding board of advisors. Together, we represent nearly 300 years of combined industry experience, bringing unparalleled expertise to workforce development in cybersecurity.

"We scan the internet and create robust curriculums utilizing reputable complimentary training resources that would otherwise cost between $8,525 and $26,640 for online education or $9,000 to $20,000 for an online cyber cert bootcamp."

Our comprehensive program addresses the critical gap in cybersecurity education by providing industry-vetted content from leading organizations including Cisco, Microsoft, AWS, RangeForce, EvolveSecurity, AttackIQ, Idaho National Labs, Qualys, and Fortinet.

As leaders in this space, we're committed to burning down these broken processes and kindling the rebirth of a new approach to cybersecurity workforce development. We're reshaping how the industry identifies, molds, and acquires a new breed of talent.

Our Comprehensive Program

A two-part curriculum designed to transform careers and build cybersecurity expertise from the ground up.

Part 1: Career Foundation

4-5 Days

Candidates explore and research the 52 cybersecurity roles presented by the National Initiative for Cybersecurity Careers and Study. This phase includes:

  • Comprehensive role analysis and career pathway exploration
  • Myers-Briggs personality assessment
  • Cybersecurity aptitude evaluation by CyberKnights
  • Identification of 3-5 aligned career paths
  • Career transition planning and goal setting

Part 2: Technical Mastery

380 Hours (12 Weeks)

Intensive, curated training featuring content from industry leaders. This comprehensive phase includes:

  • Modular training from Cisco, Microsoft, AWS, and other industry leaders
  • Hands-on labs and practical exercises
  • Individual and group mentorship opportunities
  • One-on-one sessions with advisory board members
  • LinkedIn networking assistance and career support
  • Peer collaboration and knowledge sharing

What is a "Whole Cyber Human?"

A Whole Cyber Human is defined as a person who has built foundational and practical knowledge holistically in the information technology and cybersecurity space. They are immersed into the full spectrum of cyber and empowered with training to be force multipliers.

They are career pivoters, transitioning veterans, or potential service members who have obtained critical mid-senior level soft and hard skills highly sought after in the job market. They are our children in school who have built efficiency in using technology. They are the key to securing the next 20 years.
