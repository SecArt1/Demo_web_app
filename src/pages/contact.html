Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for your programs?

Our programs are completely free and open to veterans, military families, and first responders. Simply fill out our contact form or reach out directly to get started on your cybersecurity journey.

How long are the training programs?

Our comprehensive program consists of two parts: a 4-5 day career foundation phase and a 380-hour (12-week) technical mastery phase. All training is self-paced to accommodate your schedule.

Are programs available globally?

Yes! Our programs are available globally through our online platform, offering tier-based cohorts to ensure you get the support and community you need regardless of your location.

Do you offer corporate partnerships?

Absolutely! We welcome partnerships with organizations looking to support veteran education or enhance their cybersecurity workforce development. Contact us to discuss collaboration opportunities.

What certifications can I earn?

Our curriculum prepares you for industry-standard certifications from leading organizations like Cisco, Microsoft, AWS, and many others. We provide guidance on certification paths aligned with your career goals.

Is there ongoing support?

Yes! We provide mentorship opportunities, one-on-one sessions with advisory board members, LinkedIn networking assistance, and ongoing career support throughout your journey.
